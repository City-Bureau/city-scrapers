Devon Avenues SSA #43 Commissioners Meeting

  • Share:

Name: Devon Avenues SSA #43 Commissioners Meeting
Date: September 16, 2019
Time: 2:30 PM - 4:30 PM CDT
Event Description:
Devon Avenues SSA #43

Devon Avenues SSA #43 Commissioners meet monthly to discuss initiatives, review programs and budgets for Devon Avenues SSA #43. These meetings are open to the public. 

Only the Mayor appointed and vetted Commissioners may make motions and vote.

Meetings are held every third Monday of the month  at 3 p.m. Alderman Silverstein's office,
2949 W. Devon Ave.  Except for the January and February dates, meetings will be held on Jan, 14th and Feb, 11th same time and location.

 Meeting locations, dates and times are subject to change, please check back one week prior to the meeting to verify information.

More information contact Rogers Park Business Alliance, 773-508-5885 or visit, rpba.org/ssa-43.

November 2017 Regular Board Meeting

11/2/2017 9:00 AM
11/2/2017 12:00 PM

9:00 a.m. - 12:00 noon
Full Description
​The November regularly scheduled Board meeting of the Board of Trustees of Community College District No. 508, County of Cook and State of Illinois, has been scheduled for Thursday, November 2, 2017 at 9:00 a.m., District Office, 226 W. Jackson Boulevard, Room 300, Chicago, IL 60606.

Any individual planning to attend this meeting who will need an accommodation under the Americans with Disabilities Act should notify the Board Office, at (312) 553-2515 or at requesttospeak@ccc.edu.

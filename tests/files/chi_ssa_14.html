Public Meetings

The SSA Governing Commission meetings are organized on a regular basis (3-4 times a year or as needed). While serving as a means of public accountability and important decision-making, these meetings also provide an opportunity for interaction with our major stakeholders - the Marquette Park community. The meetings are always open to the public and all the residents are strongly encouraged to attend.

In this section of SSA# 14 website we will provide a tentative meeting schedule and cover major accomplishments and points of discussion on our recent meetings. We will also post here the minutes of each meeting, the original recorded versions of which (both in writing and by means of a sound recording device) and stored at LHS headquarters in Chicago.

Note:The minutes of this meeting will be released and posted online after their draft is fully approved by the Commission.

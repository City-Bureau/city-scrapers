About Us

The Cook County Land Bank Authority (CCLBA) works to reduce and return vacant land and abandoned buildings back into reliable and sustainable community assets.
ABOUT US

Apply/Buy Property

Learn about our inventory of property, including the property type, location, cost to acquire, and other relevant information. Our inventory changes daily.
APPLY/BUY PROPERTY

Work with Us

CCLBA partners with individuals, community organizations, nonprofits, for-profit firms, municipal governments, and others to achieve our mission. Learn more.
WORK WITH US

CCLBA Data

CCLBA and our partners use a variety of data to make strategic decisions on acquisitions, evaluate neighborhood and regional real estate conditions, and conduct research.
CCLBA DATA

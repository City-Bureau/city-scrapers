      Board Meeting Schedule 2021

      Chicago Public Library Board of Directors meetings are held each quarter. Meetings will be held virtually until further notice. A livestream link will be added to the board meeting agenda on the CPL website the morning of the meeting.

      Tuesday, January 26, 9 a.m.
      Virtual meeting

      Tuesday, May 18, 9 a.m.
      Virtual meeting

      Tuesday, September 21, 9 a.m.
      Virtual meeting

      Tuesday, November 16, 9 a.m.
      Virtual meeting

      Public Comment

      • Written statements are the preferred method of public comment and will be accepted up to 24 hours in advance of the meeting at ebjones@chipublib.org.
      • Members of the public wishing to speak during the public comment portion of the meeting must complete a speaking request form. The form will be included on the Board of Directors agenda on the CPL website. Forms should be submitted by 10 a.m. the Monday prior to the meeting.
      • A maximum of 5 members of the public will be permitted to comment. Selected speakers will be chosen on a first-come, first-serve basis and will receive emailed instructions on how to provide up to 2 minutes of comment.

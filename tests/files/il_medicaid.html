Meeting Schedule

Page Content

Medicaid Advisory Committee (MAC): Meetings will take place every other month in calendar year 2014, unless otherwise noted. All meetings take place in the department's videoconference rooms. Participants attend the meetings in either the Chicago or Springfield location. MAC meetings are open to the public.

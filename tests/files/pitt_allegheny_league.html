ALOM Can Administer Oaths of Office

Before assuming the duties of office, newly elected and re-elected municipal officials must take an oath of office to support the Constitution of the United States, the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania and to perform duties of office with fidelity.
Recently, the ALOM Staff became certified to provide notarial services, including administering oaths of office. Contact our office should you need assistance with administering the oath of office for any new or re-elected officials in January.

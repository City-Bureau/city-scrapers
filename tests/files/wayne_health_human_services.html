Health & Human Services

This committee reviews matters pertaining to general community and public health, including the Department of Health, Veterans and Community Wellness; boards of health and social services; and youth-related matters, including the Head Start program, other juvenile services and juvenile detention facilities.

Chair: Tim Killeen
Vice-Chair: Terry Marecki
Member: Glenn S. Anderson
Member: Alisha Bell
Member: Reggie Reg Davis

All meetings held in the 7th floor meeting room, Guardian Building, 500 Griswold, Detroit, unless otherwise indicated

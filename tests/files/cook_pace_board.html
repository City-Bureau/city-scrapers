Calendar of Events

Regular monthly meetings of the Pace Board of Directors, for the coming year will be held in the Board Room at the principal office of Pace at 550 W. Algonquin Rd., Arlington Heights, IL 60005.

NOTES:

  • Advance notice will be given in the event of any change in date, time or location of a meeting.
  • The Meeting Agenda will be provided here two days prior to a Board meeting.
  • The Audit Committee Agenda will be provided here two days prior to an Audit Committee meeting.
  • The Meeting Minutes are available below.
2019 Meetings
Notice is hereby given of the change in date for the regularly scheduled January 9, 2019 meeting of the Board of Directors of Pace, the Suburban Bus Division of the Regional Transportation Authority. The meeting will be held on January 16, 2019 beginning at 4:30 p.m.
Public meetings of the Pace Board of Directors take place at Pace's offices at 550 West Algonquin Road, Arlington Heights, Illinois.
For further information call the Board Secretary at 847-228-2497.

About
​Since 2009, the 51st Street Business Association has served as a strategic partner and resource to Chicago’s Washington Park, Bronzeville and Grand Boulevard neighborhoods located on the city’s South Side––helping small businesses, entrepreneurs, nonprofits and residents move the neighborhood forward into the 21st century while preserving its historical past. Let us do the legwork, remove the hassles and aid your path to success because when you benefit, we all do.
— Sandra Bivens, Executive Director
The 51st Street Business Association is a non profit 501 c3 working to make a difference in a distressed urban community.  We were founded in April 2009 by businesses and residents in the 51st street corridor.  We are piloting economic development projects utilizing city, state, federal,  philanthropic  and community resources.

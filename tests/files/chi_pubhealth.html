2018 Board of Health Meetings

The Chicago Board of Health is scheduled to meet on the third Wednesday of each month from 9:00am-10:30am. The meetings are held at the Chicago Department of Public Health, DePaul Center, 333 S. State Street, 2nd Floor Board Room. The specific dates, by month, for 2018 are:

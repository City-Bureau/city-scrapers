CHA encourages community members to stay informed on current issues and business actions pertaining to public housing in Chicago. In accordance with the Illinois Open Meetings Act, CHA notifies residents and the general public of upcoming Board and public meetings, and posts announcements and agenda items to this page. 

Check this page frequently to find updated items and notices.

If you need foreign language interpretation services during the meeting or if you need assistance interpreting this document e-mail chala@thecha.org

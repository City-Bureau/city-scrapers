2024 Board of Health Meetings

The Chicago Board of Health meets at 9:00 am on the last Wednesday of every month unless otherwise indicated. The Board of Health currently meets in a “hybrid” format. Board members are required to attend in-person. The general public may attend in-person or via remote means.

  • Meeting Location: 111 W Washington Street, Conference Room 1212

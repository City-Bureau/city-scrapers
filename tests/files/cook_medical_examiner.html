Notice of Medical Examiner’s Advisory Committee

The purpose of the Cook County Medical Examiner’s Advisory Committee is to make recommendations to the Cook County Board of Commissioners for improving operations of the Office of the Cook County Medical Examiner’s Office and service to the residents of Cook County.

NOTICE OF MEETING AND AGENDA

Date: Friday, January 18, 2019

Time: 11:00 AM-12:30PM

Location: Cook County Medical Examiner’s Office

2121 W Harrison

Chicago, IL 60612

Agenda

  • Roll Call
  • Review and approval of General Meeting Minutes from July 20 and October 19, 2018
  • Public Comment
  • Reports from:
    • Medical Examiner's Office: Deputy Chief Medical Examiner, Eimad Zakariya, MD
      • General Report
    • Vice-Chairman, Jason Moran
      • Committee Attendance & Annual Report
  • Old Business
  • New Business
  • Adjournment - Next Meeting: April 19, 2019

Notice: This agenda conforms to the requirements of Section 2.02 of the Illinois Open Meetings Act (5 ILCS 120/02). Any individual planning to attend who will need an accommodation under the Americans with Disabilities Act should contact Antonia Mayorga at 312.666.0500 or via email at antonia.mayorga@cookcountyil.gov

This communication is for informational purposes only.

Discussion of this communication by more than a majority of a quorum of committee members “by video or audio conference, telephone call, electronic means (such as, without limitation, electronic mail, electronic chat, and instant messaging), or other means of contemporaneous interactive communication” could be deemed a meeting under the Illinois Open Meetings Act.

Medical Examiner’s Advisory Committee Calendar

OFFICE OF THE MEDICAL EXAMINER COUNTY OF COOK

MEDICAL EXAMINER ADVISORY COMMITTEE

PUBLIC MEETINGS CALENDAR

Notice is hereby given that the Medical Examiner Advisory Committee will meet on the following dates at the lower level of the Office of the Medical Examiner, 2121 West Harrison Street, Chicago, Illinois 60612:

Friday, January 18, 2019

Friday, April 19, 2019

Friday, July 19, 2019

Friday, October 18, 2019

Friday, January 17, 2020

Each meeting will convene at 11:00 a.m. and conclude by 12:30 p.m. and will be open to the public in accordance with the Open Meetings Act, 5 ILCS 120, and will be accessible to individuals with disabilities in compliance with pertinent state and federal laws upon notification of anticipated attendance. Individuals needing special accommodations who plan to attend the meeting should contact Antonia Mayorga at the Office of the Medical Examiner by telephone at (312) 666.0500 or via email at antonia.mayorga@cookcountyil.gov

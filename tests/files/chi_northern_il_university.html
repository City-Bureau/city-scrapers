Information about meetings of the Board of Trustees is provided below. All members of the public are welcome to attend.

For regular board meetings, and unless noted otherwise, Board meetings begin at 1 p.m. and are held at Northeastern Illinois University, 5500 North St. Louis Avenue, Chicago, Ill., 60625. Confirmed meeting locations and times may be found on the agendas for posted meetings on the Board Meeting Materials page.

All meetings may include an Executive Session if voted upon by the Board in accordance with the Illinois Open Meetings Act.

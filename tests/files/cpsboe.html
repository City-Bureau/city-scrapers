Advance registration will be open the Monday preceding the Board meeting at 10:30 a.m. and close Tuesday at 5:00 p.m., or until all slots are filled or otherwise noted.  Advance registration is available for speakers and observers.

You can advance register via:

  • Online: www.cpsboe.org (recommended)
  • Phone: (773)553-1600
  • In Person: 1 North Dearborn Street, Suite 950

Board meetings begin at 10:30 a.m., unless otherwise noted. The public participation segment of the meeting will begin at the time indicated on the meeting agenda and proceed for no more than 60 speaking slots for up to two hours.

Further, let the official record reflect that the 2017-2018 Planning Calendar has been prepared in accordance with the Illinois Open Meetings Act.

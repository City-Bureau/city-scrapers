About the Committee

  • The Belle Isle Park Advisory Committee advises the state on implementation of improvements, master planning and public safety for Belle Isle Park.

    Contact:

    Renee Parker
    Phone: 517-284-6135

  • Committee Members
  • Bylaws

Grand Prix Info

  • Representatives from the Detroit Grand Prix on July 13, 2018, presented a plan seeking to continue using Belle Isle Park as a race venue. The DNR has completed its evaluation and has offered a counter-proposal that supports hosting the race at the island park, provided certain requirements are met. A summary of that counter-proposal is available below. In the meantime, we encourage people to review these documents and continue to share their input.

    • Summary: DNR Grand Prix counter-proposal July 27, 2018
    • Grand Prix DNR Renewal Proposal Presentation July 13, 2018
    • Outline of event extension process
    • DRAFT Detroit Grand Prix proposal

    Public feedback
    The DNR will accept public comments, questions and other feedback via email at DNR-GrandPrix-Proposal@michigan.gov through Aug. 2, 2018. Also, a special meeting of the Belle Isle Park Advisory Committee will be held Thursday, Aug. 2, at 6 p.m. at the Belle Isle Nature Center (176 Lakeside Drive in Belle Isle Park), where DNR staff will review and discuss the future of the Detroit Grand Prix at Belle Isle Park, and the public will have the opportunity for comment.

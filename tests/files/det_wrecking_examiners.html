2018 Notice of Wrecking Board of Examiners Meetings
In accordance with the OPEN MEETINGS ACT OF MICHIGAN (Act 267 of 1976), please be advised of the regular meetings of the Building, Safety Engineering & Environmental Department's Wrecking Board of Examiners schedule as follows at 2 Woodward Avenue, Coleman A. Young Municipal Center Room 412, Detroit, Michigan 48226

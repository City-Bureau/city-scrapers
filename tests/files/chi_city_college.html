December 2018 Regular Board Meeting

12/6/2018 9:00 AM
12/6/2018 12:00 PM

9:00 AM to 12:00 noon
Full Description
​The December regularly scheduled meeting of the Board of Trustees of Community College District No. 508, County of Cook and State of Illinois, has been scheduled for Thursday, December 6, 2018 at 9:00 a.m., Harold Washington College, 30 E. Lake Street, 11th Floor, Chicago, IL 60601.

Any individual planning to attend this meeting who will need an accommodation under the Americans with Disabilities Act should notify the Board Office, at (312) 553-2515 or at requesttospeak@ccc.edu.

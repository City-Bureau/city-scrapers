Board Meetings

Our Board Meetings, unless otherwise noted, take place on Thursdays at 7:00 PM at our office, located at 117 Northfield Road, Northfield.

Agendas for board meetings are posted 48 hours before the meeting. Board meeting minutes are posted within 10 days of approval by the board and available for 60 days after posting.

Changes to the schedule will be posted 10 days before the new meeting date. If a special meeting is required, a notice will be posted online 48 hours before the meeting date.
